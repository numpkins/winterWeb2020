POOBRAIN

May 5, 2019

i want to write music, right?

i want to write poetry, right?

in a way, the internet has scourged my voice, non-sequiturs,

comedy, irony, in short blips, if it makes people laugh, cool,

i’ve trained for years to make people laugh.

i’ve been torn apart, re-stuffed, shattered, re-mended, loved tenderly, un-loved, dozens of times

how do i train for that?

i’m an artist, i write comics, i make t-shirts, i make people laugh

laughter is valuable, but at this point, for whom?

making people happy makes me happy, it makes me loved, which makes me happy

but when i’m not happy, what then?

i’m an artist, i’m intelligent, i think? maybe in my teens, maybe the funnies have rot my brain since then

i have lots of words that float around in my head,

a lot of burning, flesh-rotting words, head-smashing words, skin-cutting words,

that show themselves through my actions

like a caveman, almost, i suppose? bad word in brain. don’t know how move mouth. hurt self. unga bunga.

comprehend, even

your own sense of being

scared I will forget everything I am capable of?

do I want to be king?
